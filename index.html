About the Firm

A research practice built
for real-world delivery

RSS TechLabs, Research and Software Solutions is an R&D company founded by Shiva Kumar in Hyderabad, Telangana. We operate at the intersection of rigorous research and disciplined engineering: emerging technologies are studied deeply, then translated into software, products and programs that create measurable value.

From intelligent systems powered by Machine Learning and Generative AI to secure, production-grade software, our work is guided by a single conviction, the gap between what is taught in classrooms and what industry demands can, and should, be closed.
